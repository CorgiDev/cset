Share Assessment with DHS

Interested to know your score compared to your peers? Would you like to be part of your industry cybersecurity efforts? If you submit your assessment information to DHS you can see your score compared to your industry and sector, but also help DHS to make informed decisions with regard to how they support your sector and industry. Contributing your data helps your whole industry to improve. DHS uses this information to inform their decisions with regard to how they assist your industry, and allocate their own internal resources and determine priorities.

There is a difference between anonymous and anonymous with login. In both cases we anonymize your data. DHS will not link your submission back to you. In the login case we provide the ability for you to see your results score compared to your industry and sector average.

Clicking “Submit Anonymous” will submit the data shown in the table below (question identifiers, questions, and answers) with no user identifying information.

Clicking “Submit with Login” will submit the same data, but will be associated with an anonymous user identifier accessible only to you.
